What We Do

Entrepreneurship Cell(E-cells) is set up to foster entrepreneurship among the students. It is like a club, run by students and driven by the management of the institutes. Students learn important & useful trait about entrepreneurship.

Our Vision

Entrepreneurship is the process of designing, launching and running a new business, which is often initially a small business. The people who create these businesses are called entrepreneurs.
